| Challenges and Opportunities of Edge AI for Next-Generation Implantable BMIs

[Neurotechnology, Integrated Circuit Design]

M. Shaeri et al.,"Challenges and Opportunities of Edge AI for Next-Generation Implantable BMIs", IEEE 4th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence Circuits and Systems (AICAS), 2022, pp. 190-193, doi: 10.1109/AICAS54282.2022.9870008.


Summary

Definition of Brain Machine Interfaces (BMI)?
Brain implantable systems that close the loop from sensing to action.

What are the relevant trade-off when developing BMI devices?
Trade-off between spatiotemporal resolution and invasiveness, the intracortical and cortical recordings of brain activity are widely used in BMI applications.
